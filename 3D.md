# 3D-Printing

## Material

[E-Sun SilkPla (Multicolor [red & blue])](https://www.amazon.de/eSUN-Filament-Zweifarbiger-Drucker-Ma%C3%9Fgenauigkeit/dp/B0B1D697RR/)

### Print settings

**Parameter**   | **Value**
---             |---
TemperatureH    | 210°C
TemperatureB    | 67°C
Pattern         | Zigzag
Infill          | 15%
Layerhight      | 0.16mm

### Technical properties

* Breaks easily
* Does not look very nice at the top and bottom
* Some stringing
